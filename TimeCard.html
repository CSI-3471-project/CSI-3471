Welcome to the Bear Trade Platform Project Page

The Bear trade platform will provide the users the platform to sell the good(textbook, furniture, etc) and post the need for the community. Selling can either be achieved by placing the bid or regular selling posts. There should be various payment methods supported(cash, and creadit card). Sellers and buyers can evaluate each other after the trade, and the evaluation will be counted towards credit. Users' profiles can be viewed by other users in the community.

Our team uses this site to collaborate, share updates, host files, and provide information for the status of the trade platform as it is in development and beyond. Code and relevant files can be viewed here or downloaded directly
